Submit your Film

You can submit your animated short film or virtual reality animation project to Animation Nights New York via FilmFreeway. ANNY has rolling deadlines. Film submission is currently free.

 

ANNY Best of Fest is our annual festival held Sept 29-30 in NYC. Our ANNY Best of Fest winners are selected by a panel of animation industry judges from the films selected for our monthly curated events. Visit our ANNY Best of Fest page for more details.
